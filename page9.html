Who We Are

We are a group of likeminded, media professionals, assisting international media houses in making world class news reports and documentaries, since 2001.

On demand of our some of valued clients, we have recently included tourism in our portfolio, with a mission to provide affordable but luxurious and safe travel.

We’ve put tedious efforts to finalise our itineraries, accommodation and transport. Our experience and extensive network of contacts mean that we can offer comfortable and exciting travel options – and take you on unforgettable journeys.

Traveling to Pakistan is one of the most challenging decisions for most of the non green passport holders. But still every year Pakistan’s stunning diversified landscape attracts millions of foreigners.

According to Pakistan Tourism Development Corporation (PTDC) 1.75 million tourists visited Pakistan in 2017 and the trend is rising.

Cost, lack of information and safety concerns not only stops foreigners but also restrict many of the locals from exploring the vibrant turquoise waters, icy peaks glaciers and gorges, the fairytale places, vast deserts, the hidden natural splendors of the nature, or the rich cultural heritage of Pakistan.

Contrarily to general perception, the law and order situation has much improved in Pakistan during recent years. No wonder, Forbes, the leading global media company has ranked Pakistan among the ten coolest places to visit in 2019.
